# Toast (Snack Bar)

**Link**: [Snack Bar Documentation](https://zeroheight.com/8ei12wwuw/p/653285-button) (Note: Verify URL, extracted via Search)

## Usage
Feedback on user operations. Low priority, non-blocking.

## Anatomy
1.  **Container**: Rectangular background.
2.  **Message**: Text label (Max 2 lines).
3.  **Action (Optional)**: Single text button (e.g. "Undo").
4.  **Close Button**: Icon.

## Behavior
-   **Position**: Fixed at bottom of page.
-   **Auto-dismiss**: Disappears after **7 seconds** (Default).
-   **Interruption**: Disappears on page change or interaction elsewhere.

## Dos & Don'ts
-   **Do**: Keep text short.
-   **Do**: Ensure high contrast for action buttons.
-   **Don't**: Block other important UI.
