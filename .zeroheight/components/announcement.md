# Announcement

**Link**: [Announcement Documentation](https://zeroheight.com/8ei12wwuw/p/481dc6-announcement)

## Usage
Broadcast important info at the top (or bottom) of the view.

## Styles
1.  **Primary**: General information (Blue).
2.  **Warning**: Alerts/Attention needed (Orange/Yellow).
3.  **Success**: Confirmation (Green).

## Structure
-   **Icon**: Solid icon, context-specific.
-   **Title** (Optional): Key message.
-   **Content**: Supplementary details.
-   **Close Button** (Optional): "Flat Button" variant.

## Placement
-   **Fixed**:
    -   Can be placed **Above Header** or **Below Header**.
    -   *Recommendation*: **Above Header** is more logical for system-wide alerts.
    -   **Web**: Full width.
    -   **App**: Full width. NOTE: Fixed announcements on mobile reduce viewport space significantly.

## Behavior
-   **Fixed**: Stays visible while scrolling.
-   **Non-fixed**: Scrolls with content.
-   **Text Wrapping**:
    -   **Web**: Can expand height.
    -   **App**: Recommended character limits to prevent excessive height.

## Dos & Don'ts
-   **Do**: Use "Warning" style for system maintenance or critical updates.
-   **Do**: Use "Primary" for feature announcements.
-   **Don't**: Clutter the screen with multiple stacked announcements.
