# Flash Notice

## Usage
Passive notification for system status or promotions.
- Less intrusive than Alerts, more persistent/noticeable than Snack Bars.

## Anatomy
- **Container**: Rectangular card.
- **Title**: Bold, logical.
- **Message**: Optional explanation.
- **Dismiss**: 'X' icon.

## Specs
- **Desktop Width**: Fixed **280px**.
- **Mobile Width**: Fixed **220px**.
- **Position**: Fixed **Bottom Left**.
    - Margin: 16px from edges.
- **Duration**: Auto-dismiss after **5 seconds**.

## Behavior
- **Transition**: Slide up/fade in.
- **Dismissal**: Manual 'X' click or timeout.
