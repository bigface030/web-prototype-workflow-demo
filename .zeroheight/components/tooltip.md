# Tooltip

**Link**: [Tooltip Documentation](https://zeroheight.com/8ei12wwuw/p/653285-button) (Note: Verify URL, extracted via Search)

## Usage
Floating supplementary info triggered by Hover or Click.

## Anatomy
1.  **Beak**: Arrow pointing to trigger.
2.  **Container**: Background box.
3.  **Content**: Text info.

## Placement
-   **Position**: Top, Bottom, Left, Right.
-   **Alignment**: Start, Center (Default), End.

## Dos & Don'ts
-   **Do**: Use for brief explanations.
-   **Don't**: Use **Popover** if content has links or long description.
