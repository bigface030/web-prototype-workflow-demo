## Language Support
Uses Google Web Fonts (Noto Sans series) for multi-language support from Desktop to Mobile App.

*   **Traditional Chinese**: Noto Sans TC
*   **Simplified Chinese**: Noto Sans SC
*   **Japanese**: Noto Sans JP
*   **Korean**: Noto Sans KR
*   **Thai**: Sarabun (Noto Sans Thai not used)
*   **English**: Noto Sans
*   **Cantonese**: Noto Sans HK

**Special Case**: Date Picker numbers always use **Noto Sans** (no hook on digit '1').

## Fallback Stacks (CSS)
*   **TC**: `'Noto Sans', 'Noto Sans TC', sans-serif, 'PingFangTC', 'Microsoft JhengHei'`
*   **JP**: `'Noto Sans', 'Noto Sans JP', sans-serif, 'Meiryo', 'MS Gothic'`
