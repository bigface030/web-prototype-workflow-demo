# Writing Formats

**Link**: [Writing Formats Documentation](https://zeroheight.com/8ei12wwuw/p/63af4d-writing-formats)

## Spacing Rules
### Chinese & English/Numbers
-   **Rule**: Must add a **half-width space** between Chinese characters and English words or Numbers.
    -   ✅ "AsiaYo 提供最優質..."
    -   ✅ "超過 60,000 多間..."
-   **Exception**: No space if the English/Number is next to a **full-width punctuation**.
    -   ✅ "下載 AsiaYo 的 App，使用..." (Comma is full-width)

## Punctuation
### Colons (冒號)
-   **Chinese**: Full-width `：` (e.g., 客服信箱：)
-   **English**: Half-width `:` (e.g., mail: )

### Double Quotes (雙引號)
-   **Chinese**: Use `「` and `」` OR `“` and `”`.
    -   If using `“ ”`, add a **space** between the Chinese text and the quotes.
    -   Example: `請至少設定 “功能一”`
-   **English**: Use standard `“` `”` with **no extra spaces**.
