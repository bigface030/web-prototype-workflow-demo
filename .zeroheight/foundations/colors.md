# Product Colors

**Link**: [Product Colors Documentation](https://zeroheight.com/8ei12wwuw/p/93afb0-product-colors)

## About (Usage)
The color system is divided into functional groups.
- **Texts**: White, Black, Gray 1-4, Primary 1-3.
- **Backgrounds**: White, Light Gray, Primary 1-2.
- **Borders**: Light Gray, Gray, Primary.

## Functional Palettes
Defined with SCSS tokens (e.g., `ark-success-dark`).

1.  **Primary**: Ark Blue family (Dark, Base, Light).
2.  **Success**: Green family (Dark, Base, Light, Lighter).
3.  **Warning**: Yellow family (Dark, Base, Light, Lighter).
4.  **Danger**: Orange family (Dark, Base, Light, Lighter).
